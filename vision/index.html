Three completely different ways into Google.

Concrete, digger hire and bamboo removal don't share a customer. The person ordering a concrete pump is not the person Googling how to stop bamboo lifting their patio.

Cobra ConcreteReady-mix, pumping and volumetric concrete. A live, indexed brand.

Each one already ranks. Each one already rings the phone. That's not marketing spend, that's inherited value.

The temptation

The easy move is to merge them into one website.

Do that, and two of the three brands disappear from Google overnight. You'd be paying to throw away search visibility you already own.

Instead

Keep three specialist front doors.

Each brand stays the obvious answer to its own search. A focused bamboo site wins the bamboo job. A general services menu doesn't.
