Why bamboo is different

The problem is under the ground

Cutting bamboo back does nothing. It spreads through underground rhizomes that can run metres from the original plant, lifting paving, pushing through boundaries and even coming up in a neighbour's garden.

That is why a proper job means excavating the root system, not just clearing what you can see.

Lifts patios & driveways as rhizomes push up under hard surfaces
Crosses boundaries into neighbouring gardens and causes disputes
Threatens a sale flagged in surveys and worrying to buyers
